FOOD GALLERY

Banana split ice cream served on a banana sliced in half lengthwise and usually garnished with flavored syrups, fruits, nuts, and whipped cream.

Kabab A kebab is pieces of meat or vegetables grilled on a long thin stick, or slices of grilled meat served in pitta bread.

Berger a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun roll.

Bread toasted Toast is bread that has been browned by radiant heat. The browning is the result of a Maillard reaction, altering the flavor of the bread and making it firmer so that it is easier to spread toppings on it.
